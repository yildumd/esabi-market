About E-Sabi Market

Connecting Traditional Markets to Modern Consumers

E-Sabi Market is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between traditional open markets and the modern digital economy. We bring the vibrant experience of Nigerian open markets to your fingertips.

Our mission is to empower local vendors while providing customers with unprecedented access to fresh, affordable produce from the comfort of their homes.

🛒

Our Vision

To revolutionize how Nigerians access market goods while supporting local vendors and reducing food waste.
